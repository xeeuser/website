Common Questions

What Clients Ask Before Starting

How long does a typical project take? +
Most projects run 8–20 weeks depending on scope. Mobile apps typically 10–14 weeks; SaaS portals 12–20 weeks; automation projects 6–10 weeks. We give you a firm timeline in the Discovery Sprint.
Do you work with early-stage startups? +
We work best with Series A or late pre-seed companies that have a validated problem and defined product roadmap. If you are still validating your idea, we will tell you to do that first — and come back when you are ready.
What happens after launch? +
Every project includes 30+ days of post-launch support at no extra cost. We monitor performance, fix any production issues, and hand over full documentation. After that, we offer ongoing retainer arrangements for clients who want continued support.
How do we communicate during the project? +
Dedicated Slack channel, weekly video syncs, and a shared Linear board so you can track every task in real time. You will never have to chase us for an update.
Can we start without a full spec? +
Absolutely. The Discovery Sprint exists precisely to turn a rough idea into a precise specification. You need a problem and a budget — we will help shape the rest.
